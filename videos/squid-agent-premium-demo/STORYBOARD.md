## Video direction

Palette system: use the Broadside frame system remixed to Squid—near-black `ink-black` as the dominant register, off-white `cream` for load-bearing copy, and Squid blue `fire-orange` as the only accent. Keep the screenshots' own dark navy surfaces intact. DM Sans display type, mono labels, 1px rails, selection brackets, and chapter numerals make the film feel editorial rather than like a website carousel.

Motion grammar: every screenshot is treated as a camera stage, never a flat image. Use a masked establish, a precise crop or focus pull, a VO-cued proof reveal in the back half, and a clean held read. Entrances settle with smooth long-tail motion; within-shot seams are velocity matched. No bounce, no late camera drift, and no independent floating-card motion. Burned-in sentence-case captions sit in the reserved bottom 17% band without obscuring product evidence.

Rhythm: Frame 1 opens with controlled tension. Frames 2–4 accelerate through product, plan, and visible build. Frame 5 is the deliberate trust breather. Frame 6 regains precise interaction energy. Frame 7 expands into ownership. Frame 8 resolves slowly and holds long enough for the URL to register.

Negative list: no old chat UI, HeyGen presenter, stock footage, fake terminal chrome, invented product states, rainbow gradients, purple AI fog, rounded floating feature cards, lazy breathing, slideshow front-loading, screensaver motion, or unreadably tiny text. Keep all load-bearing proof above the caption band.
