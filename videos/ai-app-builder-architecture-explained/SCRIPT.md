# SCRIPT — Squid Agent Product Explainer

**Voice:** HeyGen warm, conversational English narrator
**Voice settings:** natural pace · crisp phrasing · expressive but grounded emphasis
**Voice direction:** Speak to a smart, nontechnical viewer. Sound like a trusted product guide: curious at the opening, reassuring through the workflow, and confident at the close. Leave a small breath between each phrase separated by an em dash.

---

## Line 1 — Your idea should not stay an idea (Frame 1)

**Time:** 0.0 – 6.0s
**Delivery:** Open with an inviting question; lift slightly on “real app.”

    What if your app idea did not have to stay in a notebook—or wait months to become a real app?

## Line 2 — Meet Squid Agent (Frame 2)

**Time:** 6.0 – 14.0s
**Delivery:** Warm reveal; make “plain English” and “working React app” feel effortless.

    Meet Squid Agent. Describe what you want in plain English, add a screenshot or website, and watch it become a working React app.

## Line 3 — Research and plan first (Frame 3)

**Time:** 14.0 – 24.0s
**Delivery:** Friendly, guided cadence; pause briefly before the approval line.

    Squid can research current information, ask the few questions that matter, and turn your answers into a clear plan. Nothing builds until you approve it.

## Line 4 — See the build happen (Frame 4)

**Time:** 24.0 – 34.0s
**Delivery:** Build energy through the three visible outcomes; land on “together.”

    Approve the plan, and Squid builds in front of you—pages, interactions, and the real project files, all coming together.

## Line 5 — Quality you can actually see (Frame 5)

**Time:** 34.0 – 44.0s
**Delivery:** Reassuring and plainspoken; avoid sounding like a technical checklist.

    Then it checks for broken pieces, missing files, and basic accessibility problems—and shows you what passed before you move on.

## Line 6 — Change one thing, keep the rest (Frame 6)

**Time:** 44.0 – 54.0s
**Delivery:** Light, empowering tone; emphasize “one thing” and “keeps everything else.”

    Want a change? Select one part, say what should be different, and Squid updates the right place while keeping everything else intact.

## Line 7 — Keep what you made (Frame 7)

**Time:** 54.0 – 65.0s
**Delivery:** Confident payoff; slow down through the final list.

    When it feels right, share it, remix it, or download the complete project—with the code, setup instructions, and quality report included.

## Line 8 — Start building (Frame 8)

**Time:** 65.0 – 72.0s
**Delivery:** Spacious, optimistic close. Finish cleanly on the URL.

    From first idea to an app you can keep. That is Squid Agent. Start building at squidagent.app.
