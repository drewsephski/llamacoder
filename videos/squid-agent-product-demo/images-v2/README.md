Five production-ready 1920x1080 frames for the new YouTube demo. The set follows the dark Squid product system: near-black and deep navy surfaces, electric blue light, restrained glass edges, the real Squid mark, and green reserved for verified states.

## Safe use

- Preserve the 16:9 canvas and keep captions inside the lower title-safe band.
- Add narration captions, URLs, and any changing claims in the video editor rather than regenerating them into the artwork.
- Use smooth push-ins, focus pulls, line sweeps, and cursor cues. Avoid bounce, fake terminal typing, or constant ambient motion.
- The product references are the live Squid screenshots in `../screenshots/`; they should remain the truth source for future revisions.
